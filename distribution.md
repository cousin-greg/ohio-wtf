## 5. First 30 Days Action Plan

### Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

- [ ] **Day 1-2:** Set up landing page (use the copy above, deploy on a simple site — Carrd, Framer, or similar). Get a domain. Add a Calendly link for free audits.
- [ ] **Day 2-3:** Post on Reddit r/boulder (pick one of the 3 versions above). Engage genuinely with every comment.
- [ ] **Day 3:** Post on Nextdoor as described above.
- [ ] **Day 3-4:** Join 3 Boulder Facebook groups. Don't post yet — just engage and comment helpfully for a few days.
- [ ] **Day 5-7:** Identify 10 local businesses that are obvious automation candidates (use the business type list above). Research each one — find something specific to pitch.
- [ ] **Day 7:** Send cold outreach to 5 of those 10 businesses using Template A.

**Goal:** Landing page live, Reddit post up, 5 cold outreaches sent.

### Week 2: Network (Days 8-14)

- [ ] **Day 8:** Post in Facebook groups (value-first — share a free automation tip, mention you do this locally).
- [ ] **Day 9:** Attend 1 Million Cups (Wednesday). Talk to 5+ people. Collect contacts.
- [ ] **Day 10-11:** Send cold outreach to the other 5 businesses. Follow up on any Reddit DMs using Template B.
- [ ] **Day 12:** Visit 2 co-working spaces. Ask about community boards, events, lunch-and-learns.
- [ ] **Day 13-14:** Follow up on all Week 1 outreach (one gentle follow-up per person). Identify 10 more prospects.

**Goal:** 10+ conversations started, 2-3 audit calls booked.

### Week 3: Convert (Days 15-21)

- [ ] Do the free audits. Come prepared with specific recommendations and a rough quote.
- [ ] Send proposals within 24 hours of each audit call. Keep proposals to 1 page.
- [ ] Continue networking — attend another event, post on social again.
- [ ] Ask anyone you've talked to for referrals (Template C), even if they didn't buy.
- [ ] Pitch a lunch-and-learn to one co-working space for Week 4.

**Goal:** 2-3 proposals sent, 1 signed client.

### Week 4: Deliver + Compound (Days 22-30)

- [ ] Start building for your first client. Deliver fast — under-promise, over-deliver.
- [ ] Document the project (with permission) for your landing page testimonial.
- [ ] Do the lunch-and-learn if booked. Collect emails.
- [ ] Send a second round of cold outreach (10 more businesses).
- [ ] Post a "what I learned" follow-up on Reddit — people love follow-up posts.
- [ ] Apply to speak at Boulder Startup Week (if timing works) or New Tech Meetup.

**Goal:** 1 client delivering, 2 more in pipeline, referral loop started.
